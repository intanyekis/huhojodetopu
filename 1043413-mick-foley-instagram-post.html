Mick Foley Make s His First Instagram Post (Photo)

Cue the car crash theme – Mick Foley has made his way to Instagram. As promised, Mrs. Foley’s Baby Boy made his first post on the social media mammoth last night after WWE RAW and it features two of his closest friends in the business in Terry Funk and the late Owen Hart:

FIRST FOLEY POST! For my first post here…RIGHT HERE on IG, I chose one of my very favorite photos. This photo of me with Owen Hart and Terry Funk was taken in 1998 at The Mall of America. Owen’s sister Allison even made a mug for me out of this photo! Owen Hart was one of the finest people I have ever met.

I hope you enjoy my page here on Instagram. Like it if you like, follow if you like, and let your friends know I’m on here. I will do my best to make this a fun and informative place to visit.
