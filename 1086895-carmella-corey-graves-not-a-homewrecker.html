Carmella Says Shes Not A Homewrecker, Never Dated John Cena

Carmella has finally broken her silence about her relationship with Corey Graves. While she didn’t talk about whether they were an item currently (she instead said it’ll play out on Total Divas), she did address the allegations by Graves’ ex-wife, who labeled her a “homewrecker.” This was all on the latest episode of the Bellas Podcast.

“Let me just say that I would never wreck someone’s home,” said Carmella. “That is not the life I’m about. Never in a million years would I do that. I don’t care how hot the guy or how in love I think I am, that absolutely did not happen. I did not wreck a home!”

She went on to state that there are many sides to every story and that it all plays out on Total Divas this season. Carmella then addressed another rumor that she had dated John Cena. “That is the most insane rumor. It was when I had broken up with my ex and he had been seen out in a bar in Tampa, and they just assumed he was with me.”
