LA Knight To CM Punk And Randy Orton: Get In Line

LA Knight sends a message to Randy Orton and CM Punk.

At the WWE live event on December 9, LA Knight and Kevin Owens defeated Solo Sikoa and Jimmy Uso. Knight grabbed a microphone and cut a promo after the match. He thanked Owens before he told Orton and Punk to get in line. (Video courtesy of The YEAH! Revolution on Twitter).

“First and foremost, I came out here looking for The Bloodline. I get blindsided, I get beat down, and who comes out here but KO. We got a common enemy, and just for that, I will say thank you. But you talk about common enemies, and all the sudden, up shows CM Punk. Then up shows Randy Orton. All I hear is everybody gunning for Roman Reigns.

“Here’s how it boils down. If Roman Reigns finds enough tingle in his loins to actually show up, he’s got a lot of people looking for him. But guess what, boys? CM Punk, Randy Orton, everybody else, you’ve got to get in line because I ain’t asking ya, I’m telling ya whose game this is.”

Knight previously lost to Undisputed WWE Universal Champion Roman Reigns at WWE Crown Jewel. However, he has made it clear that he is not finished with The Bloodline.

CM Punk is set to reveal his decision regarding which brand he will sign with on the December 11 episode of WWE RAW. Roman Reigns is scheduled to return on the December 15 episode of WWE SmackDown.
