Kota Ibushi Apologizes For His Performance At NOAH The New Year

Kota Ibushi apologizes for his performance at NOAH The New Year and says he got scammed.

At NOAH The New Year, Kota Ibushi was sent to the hospital after his match against Naomichi Marufuji, per Sports Hochi in Japan. At the time, it was believed that Ibushi hurt both of his ankles in the bout. Throughout the contest, it seemed like Ibushi was dealing with injuries, and the match was criticized by many fans.

Kota Ibushi took to Twitter/X to comment on the situation. He stated that he was scammed by the C5 Clinic. Ibushi also apologized for the match and noted that NOAH would likely release information about his surgery. (Translation via DeepL)

“I was scammed by a place called C5 Clinic, which is a nationwide clinic that I mumbled about before,” Ibushi wrote. “Please be careful everyone.

“I think NOAH will release information about my surgery, fracture dislocation, etc. Sorry for the sorry match everyone. It is all my fault.”

WrestleZone will provide more information as it becomes available.
