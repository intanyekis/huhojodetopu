Alexa Bliss Believes Her Match With Ronda Rousey Can Headline SummerSlam

The WWE Raw Women’s Champion Alexa Bliss believes that her upcoming match with Ronda Rousey at WWE SummerSlam can headline the event.

“You have the five-time WWE Champion going up against a UFC Hall of Famer. Why not let it be the main event?” she told Mirror Sport.

“I do I believe that with the women’s evolution going the way it is, then why not? But if not, then it is still great that I get to have a match with Ronda, who is making her SummerSlam debut.”

The champion also thinks that Ronda is one of the top athletes in the world and is excited to see her progress in WWE.

“I feel like she has made such a name for herself and is one of top athletes in the world. I am exciting to see her grow as a performer and I am excited to see where she is a year from now.”
