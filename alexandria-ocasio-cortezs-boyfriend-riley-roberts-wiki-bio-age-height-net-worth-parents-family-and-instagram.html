Riley Roberts Biography – Riley Roberts Wiki

Riley Roberts is an American web developer and Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez’s boyfriend.

He graduated from Boston University with a Bachelor of arts in Sociology, then attended Boston University’s Questrom School of Business, graduating in 2012.

According to LinkedIn, Roberts is a UX Growth Consultant who “helps SaaS and subscription startups sustainably grow their monthly recurring revenue with.” His bio reads in part, “In the last 6 years, I’ve helped over 60 startups like yours improve their landing pages, digital marketing, and user experience design. Since 2013, I’ve focused my consulting efforts on subscription startups, because it’s where I’ve found I can deliver the highest value results for my clients.”

Roberts previously worked as Head of Marketing at HomeBinder in New York City. HomeBinder helps people manage the maintenance on their homes.

According to TEDxBU, Roberts gave a TED Talk on April 24, 2010. Roberts’ bio read, “Riley is a sophomore duel-degree student studying Sociology and Finance. He is passionate about all aspects of business and people. His life necessitates that he spend a significant amount of time on a computer, but, despite the way he lives, he loves to break the connection and engage others.”

Riley Roberts and Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez

Roberts and Ocasio-Cortez met while they were students at Boston University. Roberts’ and Ocasio-Cortez’s relationship was first confirmed in an October 2018 article of Ocasio-Cortez’s profile by Vogue.

Roberts, who is from Arizona, moved to New York to be with Ocasio-Cortez, and they lived in a one0bedroo apartment in the Parkchester neighborhood of the Bronx until they moved to D.C. for her congressional position.

February 15, 2019, Ocasio-Cortez confirmed that Roberts had been granted a government “house.gov” email address so he can access her congressional calendar.

According to House aides, spouses of members of Congress are afforded privileges, such as a House email address, a congressional pin to bypass security and a House license plate to enter Capitol grounds and enjoy parking privileges. While Roberts is not officially a spouse, he can have access to House email for calendar purposes, according to the office of the Chief Administrative Officer of the House.
