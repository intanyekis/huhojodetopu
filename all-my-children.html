Anna Stuart

Anna Stuart was born in Bluefield, West Virgina on November 1, 1948.

She is best known for her role as the love-to-hate Donna Love on the soap opera "Another World." In 2002 she joined the cast of All My Children as Greenlee's mother, Mary Greenlee Smythe, and quickly won over Pine Valley fans, too!

Before becoming an actress, Anna exercised horses at a horse track. She calls Robert Redford her role model.
