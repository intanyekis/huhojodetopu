Who is Antony Starr?

Antony Starr is an actor of New Zealander nationality who was seen in both TV plus film. Antony Starr is popular for featuring in Outrageous Fortune (2005–10), Banshee (2013–16), The Boys (2019–present), etc.

In 2023, he was seen as Mark in the horror thriller film Cobweb along with stars such as Lizzy Caplan, Woody Norman, Cleopatra Coleman, and Jay Rincon.

This film has got a fair response from the audience as it has ratings of 5.9 on IMDb and only 63 percent on Rotten Tomatoes.
