Synopsis

Bill Rancic is an American businessman and producer, known for winning the first season of The Apprentice. He also hosted America Now with Leeza Gibbons.

Rancic is a motivational speaker and real estate developer from Chicago, Illinois. She was also instrumental in helping to found FabFitFun, a women’s website focused on health, beauty, and fashion.

Early age and education

Bill Rancic was born as William Rancic on May 16, 1971 in Chicago, Illinois, USA. He is of white ethnicity and holds the American nationality.

Rancic is the son of Edward T. Rancic and Gail Rancic. He has three siblings, all of whom are sisters named Karen, Katie, and Beth. Rancic grew up in the suburb of Orland Park in a family of Croatian and Irish descent.

Rancic attended St. Michael’s School in Orland Park for his early education. He graduated from Carl Sandburg High School. He earned a BS from Loyola University Chicago.

Race

Bill Rancic was the first reality star hired by national television and chose to be in charge of building Trump Tower Chicago in his native Chicago, Illinois, on the site of the demolished Chicago Sun-Times building.

His other option was to oversee and manage a new Trump National Golf Course and Resort in Los Angeles, California. The Trump National course was a potential site for the show’s sixth season.

For the final challenge against competitor Kwame Jackson, Trump gave Rancic the task of directing a celebrity golf outing at Briarcliff Manor, NY, while Jackson was tasked with managing a Jessica Simpson concert .

In the final board meeting, Jackson commented that putting on a golf outing was the easier of the two tasks, prompting a disagreement with Trump’s vice president, Carolyn Kepcher, who managed a golf course when Trump hired her. Trump hired Rancic for doing a very good job.

Rancic originally said that after his one-year contract as a Donald Trump Trainee expired, Rancic would leave the job and start his own company. Instead, he chose to remain a part of the Trump Organization and, in some seasons, filled in as a judge when regular judge George Ross was away on business.

In 2008, Rancic and his wife, television host Giuliana Rancic, started a joint television production company, You and I Productions, which produces reality and scripted programming for television.

In 2010, Rancic began hosting a news show called America Now, which airs across the United States on stations owned by Raycom Media. The following year, “America Now” returned for a second season, with Leeza Gibbons joining Bill Rancic. Originally, the show ran every weekend, but when Leeza Gibbons joined the show, it moved to weekday timeslots.

Personal life

Bill Rancic married Giuliana DePandi in September 2007 in a Capri ceremony at Sancta Sophia Church in Anacapri.

His wife Giuliana DePandi is an Italian-American television personality to whom he became engaged on December 15, 2006.

On August 29, 2012, the couple welcomed a son, Edward Duke Rancic, in Denver, Colorado.

Net worth

Bill Rancic has an approximate net worth of around $5 million. He is paid an annual salary of $250,000 to Donald Trump’s companies.
