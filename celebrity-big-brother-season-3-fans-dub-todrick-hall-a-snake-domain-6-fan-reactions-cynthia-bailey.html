'Celebrity Big Brother': Todrick Hall's double-dealing has fans calling him a 'snake'

It's Sunday night which means the celebs are back with more schemes in an all-new 'Celebrity Big Brother' on CBS! It's just a few days in but everyone is already starting to play an individual game to ensure their safety.

Despite trying to cut a deal with HOH Miesha Tate, we saw Teddi Mellencamp be the first guest to be evicted from the house after being backdoored. This essentially leaves Miesha and her athletes alliance as the strongest alliance at the moment. The elimination of Teddi also means that the original and first alliance consisting of Carson Kressley, Cynthia Bailey, and Todrick Hall hardly has power with Todrick also supporting Miesha. Read on to know what fans thought of his betrayal.

After the elimination saw Teddi gone, Carson and Cynthia got together to share the sadness of their friend going. Cynthia was shocked and hurt by the fact that Todrick voted for Teddi despite them being a team. Knowing that he looked bad due to his action he decided to do damage control and went as far as telling Cynthia that he never wanted to work with Teddi despite being all chummy at the start. Stating in private that the power the HOH holds is important and that he did what's best for his game, Todrick continued his alliance with Miesha.

Cynthia too was taken aback by what he said and realized that he cannot be trusted. After being betrayed she expressed worry as this just left her and Carson together now that Shanna Moakler went over as well, after cutting a deal with Miesha to stay safe.
