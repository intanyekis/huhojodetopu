Crawford Communications Acquired in Stock Transaction

Broadcast Facilities Inc. has acquired the satellite services division of Atlanta-based Crawford Communications Inc. in a stock transaction that includes Crawfordâ€™s teleport, satellite uplink trucks, production facilities and other assets, the companies announced Jan. 21.
