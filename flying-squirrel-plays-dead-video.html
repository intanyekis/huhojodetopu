The astute rodent begins by approaching a broom and dustpan in the kitchen. The rodent appeared to be thinking, “Oh, this is it.” Then, let the cleaning item fall to the ground, sprawling out on the floor.

However, the squirrel sees that the broom was ineffective as a realistic murder weapon and chooses to try a different approach. The squirrel contemplates numerous poses while snuggling beneath the broom and lays the brush across its body.

Finally, it opens its arms wide and positions the broom across its breast, acting like a victim waiting for someone to notice.

Fortunately, its owner was filming every step of the dramatic scene. Otherwise, people may believe the squirrel perished as a result of its owner’s carelessness.

Furthermore, the Oscar-winning rodent occasionally acts out of character to test whether anyone has figured out its deceit.

The video, which was previously thought to have been posted on June 3 by an anonymous Thai person, was uploaded by Canadian user @BirrelleBee.
