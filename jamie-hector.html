Unexpected GunShot At Baby Shower

Back on July 12, 2009, an unexpected gunfight happened in East Flatbush, Brooklyn outside wife Jennifer's baby shower held by an actor Jamie.

As per NY Daily News, among other party's attender, two men Linton Williams, aged 17, and Walter Parker of 22 became the victim of the fatal gunplay. Williams died at the scene and Parker was wounded. 

After the accident, an actor Hector paid his deep condolence to the victim's families. He also said, "I would like to make it clear that the shooting incident did not take place at my home, nor did it involve me, my wife, nor any of the invited guests."
