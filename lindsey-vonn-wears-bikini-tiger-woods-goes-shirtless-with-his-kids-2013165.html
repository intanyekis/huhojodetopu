Lindsey Vonn Bikini, Tiger Woods Shirtless: Couple Hangs With His Kids

Gone fishing with Tiger, Lindsey and the cubs! About a week after their memorable date night at NYC’s star-studded Met Gala, Tiger Woods and girlfriend Lindsey Vonn were back down in the golfer’s home base state of Florida — hitting Woods’ multi-million dollar yacht, Privacy, for some fishing with Sam, 5, and Charlie, 4, his daughter and son with ex-wife Elin Nordegren.

While the world-class athletes were in their black-tie formal finery at the Metropolitan Museum of Art (and later the Top of the Standard downtown) for the May 6 Met Gala, they stripped down as they hung out on the dock. Olympic gold medalist skier Vonn, 28, showed off her toned, sexy physique in a colorful strapless bikini, while her golfer beau, 37, went shirtless in white shorts and white sneakers.

Looking more grown-up than ever, Woods’ adorable kids happily fished in the sunshine — with Sam checking out the scenery in pink sunglasses and a neon-yellow polo top. (Vonn has bonded with the kids before — and even had her brother give the tykes ski lessons in Colorado last fall.)
