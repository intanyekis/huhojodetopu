Lisa Rinna spills on Harry Hamlin and Ursula Andress 80s romance

Lisa Rinna is spilling the tea on hubby Harry Hamlin’s long-ago love life.

On Wednesday episode of “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills,” Rinna — who has been married to Hamlin since 1997 — dished about the actor’s 1980s romance with former Bond girl Ursula Andress that produced a son named Dimitri.

Rinna brought up Hamlin’s old relationship while she was in Rome with the other ladies, saying (via People) that her and Hamlin’s mansion back home looks similar architecturally because “Harry lived here for three years and he loves this … He was with Ursula Andress at the time and they had a child, she lived here. They were doing ‘Clash of the Titans’ together.”

Rinna, 57, said Andress, now 84, is the one who pursued Hamlin, now 68.

“He said she called him and said, ‘Harry, come to my room.’ And so he did, at 28 years old, he went in and she got pregnant that night,” Rinna told her pals. “And that was that, at 44. First time.”

They stayed together until 1983. Hamlin went on to marry Laura Johnson, Nicollette Sheridan and finally Rinna in 1997. He and Rinna have two grown daughters, Delilah and Amelia.

Andress told People in an old interview of her relationship with Hamlin: “I was always very possessive, and for Harry, his career is the main thing.”

“Love affairs and husbands can end, but a child is forever. Dimitri is my love now,” she said.
