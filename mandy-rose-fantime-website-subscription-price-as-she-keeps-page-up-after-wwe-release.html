Mandy Rose FanTime Website Subscription Price As She Keeps Page Up After WWE Release

This week Mandy Rose has made headlines throughout the WWE but not necessarily for the reasons, she would have preferred, know about her FanTime website page

After holding the title for well over a year, she first lost her NXT Women’s Championship. Then, after WWE officials learned that her website had content behind a paywall, she lost her contract with the company for a breach. Unfortunately, the industry lost such a great Superstar because many believed she was more than prepared for a major push on the main roster, but that is now unlikely to happen.

However, monetarily, she should be fine. Her page is sure to earn her far more money than a contract with the WWE would, especially in light of recent developments. They believed that it was outside the boundaries of her WWE deal, forcing them to break ways with the Superstar.

She has raised the monthly subscription fee for the website from $25 to $40, maximising her income. She can also give a greater variety of content now that she is free from an employment contract. Given the uproar, her popularity has never been stronger, which will only help her promote the website she has insisted will remain while thanking followers for their support.

The fact that the video she posted is supposedly not unlike the way she and other female Superstars are marketed by the firm has further incensed fans on social media.
