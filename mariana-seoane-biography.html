Mariana Seoane Bio (Wiki)

Who is Mariana Seoane?

Mariana Alejandra Seoane Gracia is a Mexican actress, model, and singer known as Mariana Seoane. Similarly, she is famous for her role in Mujer, Casos de la Vida real, Rebeca, El Chema, and many more.

She is the winner of the 2004 Revelation Popular.

She was born on 10th June 1976 in Mexico City. Similarly, she is the daughter of Jose Seoane and Stella Garcia. There is no information about her siblings and childhood.

Mariana Seoane: Professional Life, Career

In the year 1995, Mariana Seoane made her acting debut on a Televisa telenovela. Similarly, she appeared on Retrato de Familia (“Family Photo”). Her co-stars were Alfredo Adame, Helena Rojo, and Julio Bracho.

She played Aracely in Retrato de Familia. Similarly, she acted in Cancion de Amor (“Love Song”), a telenovela geared towards teenagers and young adults.

Similarly, she continued on with her acting career in the year 1997, when she filmed Mi Pequeña Traviesa (“My Little Daredevil”). She played the character of Bárbara in that telenovela.

Then she proceeded to take a two-year lay-off from telenovelas after that. But she returned in the year 1999 when she had her first opportunity of starring in a telenovela, acting as Adriana in Amor Gitano (Gypsy Love), where Islas was her co-star.

Likewise, she returned in 2001, making her comedy debut in Diseñador, Ambos Sexos (“Designer of both Sexes”) a comedy about a fashion designer who pretended to be homosexual in order to get close to women.

Further, she played Ernestina Soto in Atrévete an Olvidarme (Dare to Forget Me), a telenovela.

It was also run during the year 2001, with Jorge Salinas and Adriana Fonseca as stars. Also, she has made a total of four soap operas where Salinas also acted.
