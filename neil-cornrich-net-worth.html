Neil Cornrich

Birthname Neil Cornrich Birthplace United States of America Education University of Michigan, Ohio State University Moritz College of Law Source of Wealth NC Sports, LLC, Sports Nationality United States Country United States

American businessman Neil Cornrich is considered by Sports Illustrated to be one of the most popular and influential sports agents in 2013. He is the owner of NC Sports, LLC.

Neil Cornrich represents numerous high profile athletes such as Phil Dawson, Dallas Clark, Ted Ginn, Jr., Robert Smith, Montee Ball, and Riley Reiff.

He has established a reputation of negotiating contracts for coaches and players in sports management industry. He has helped numerous players and coaches in obtaining generous deals. These include Kirk Lowdermilk,  Dana Stubblefield, Craig Powell, Tom Tupa, Bob Stoops, Mike Wahle, Dave Zastudil, Kirk Ferentz, Al Johnson, and Marshall Yanda.

Neil Cornrich is an alumnus of University of Michigan. After completing his undergraduate degree from the university, he attended Ohio State University Moritz College of Law where he completed his law degree.
