Offset Gets Cardi Bs Name Tattooed on His Neck

A permanent declaration of love. Offset got his fianceé Cardi B‘s name tattooed on his neck.

Fans can see the Migos rapper’s seemingly real ink in a video posted by The Shade Room on Sunday, January 14. Offset, whose real name is Kiari Kendrell Cephus, got a tattoo of Cardi’s stage name “Cardi B.” Her real name is Belcalis Almanzar. The pair got engaged after Offset proposed on stage last October. 

As previously reported. Offset, 26, was accused of cheating on the “Bodak Yellow” rapper, 25, in late December when a video leaked online of a man who appeared to be Offset in a hotel room with a naked woman. Then, earlier this month, a second surfaced video appeared to show him in bed with yet another woman. Cardi took to Twitter earlier this month to respond to the drama surrounding her relationship with her fiancé.
