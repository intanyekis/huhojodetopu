PHOTOS Teen Mom Kailyn Lowrys son Isaac launches clothing line


We all know Teen Mom Kailyn Lowry’s son Isaac is a dapper little diaper dandy so it only seems natural that he would one day get his own clothing line. I just didn’t think that day would come so soon!

That’s right, Isaac is now a fashion toddler mogul as momma Kail has announced the launch of the Isaac Elliott Collection by Little Miss Cupcake! She tweeted:

According to the Facebook page for Little Miss Cupcake, founder Alexandra Lopez is extremely flexible in what she can provide as far as the cute clothing she offers. She was asked in the comments section about sizes and she stated she can do pretty much any size. The price range listed for Isaac’s collection, casual formal wear in the form of onesies, are between 13$ to $30 and you can head over to her page to find out how and where to order and pay.

Here is one more super cute pic of Isaac all dressed up in a black & red Christmas tux onesie with cuff links. Now we know how Kail’s little man rolls while he’s relaxing and watching Bubble Guppies.

All photos are via Crystal Rose Photography. You can check out additional pics of little Isaac in his I.E.C. threads via Kailyn’s official website here.
