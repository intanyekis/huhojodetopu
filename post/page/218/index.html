Catnip Tea for Cats: How to Make It, Benefits, Recipe &amp; More

published on June 11, 2024
Catnip (Nepeta cataria) is a wild perennial plant that you can find growing in almost any well-drained soil, particularly in disturbed areas and along habitat edges. The ingredient that gives it its distinctive smell is nepetalactone. This compound serves a vital function for these plants by repelling insects. Interestingly, the chemical disappeared early in the evolutionary history of the genus, only to re-emerge later.
