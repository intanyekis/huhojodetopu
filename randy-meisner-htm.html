Randy Meisner was born on March 8, 1946 (age 77) in Scottsbluff, Nebraska, United States. He is a Celebrity Musician, Songwriter. He co-founded, played bass for, and sang backing vocals for the classic rock band, The Eagles (band), the group known for songs like "Hotel California."
