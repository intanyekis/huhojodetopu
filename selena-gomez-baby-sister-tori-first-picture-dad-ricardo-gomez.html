Dad Ricky Welcomes Daughter With Wife Hollywood Life

Selena Gomez’s Father & Step Mom Give Birth To Baby Girl

Wow, Selena is SO lucky. Her family just keeps expanding more and more!

In June 2013 her mom, Mandy Teefey, gave birth to a baby girl named Gracie with her stepfather, Brian Teefey.

Now, her father Ricky and his wife have also given birth to a baby girl: Tori Gomez!

“Time flies…. It seems like just yesterday we found out we were having a baby. And now our baby Tori is 1 month. So blessed and grateful.” Ricky captioned a photo of his newborn baby girl on July 24.

Awesome! Congrats to Selena and her father — this is such happy news!

Ricky Wishes Selena Happy Birthday On Instagram

Just days before revealing that he had a second baby girl, Selena’s father paid tribute to his first on her birthday, July 22.

“To the world you are an amazing, talented, caring and beautiful young woman. To me your not only all those things, you are God’s greatest gift….. Happy Birthday Mija !! I love you,” the proud papa wrote as a caption to a photo of him and Selena, taken by the singer herself.

Such a sweet message. We’re sure Selena loved it!
