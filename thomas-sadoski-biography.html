Who is Thomas Sadoski?

Thomas Sadoski is an American stage, film, and television actor. He is best known for his series regular role as Matt Short in the American sitcom television series Life in Pieces (2015-present).

He is a highly-respected theater actor, winning an Obie Award for Distinguished Performance by an Actor for his performance in Other Desert Cities.

Thomas Sadoski: Birth Facts, Family, and Childhood

Thomas Sadoski was born on 1 July 1976, in Bethany, Connecticut, USA. He is an American by nationality and he is of mixed(Polish, Italian, German, Swedish, and English) ethnicity.

He is the son of Mark Sadoski and Carol Sadoski. His family moved to College Station, Texas when he was four years old.
