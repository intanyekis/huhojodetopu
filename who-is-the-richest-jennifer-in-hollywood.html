Who Is The Richest Jennifer In Hollywood?

When it comes to the entertainment business, it goes without saying that it's a cutthroat industry. Making it big in film, television, or music is no easy feat, especially when you're up against those with the same name.

While Hollywood is full of Chris', Kate's, and Jessica's, there is much competition when it comes to the Jennifers! From pop icon Jennifer Lopez, film star, Jennifer Garner, to TV legend, Jennifer Aniston, the list is limitless when it comes to A-list Jen's.

Although it's known that Tom Cruise lands atop the richest 'Tom' list, fans are curious who takes the win when it comes to these talented Jennifers. So, who is the richest Jennifer in Hollywood? Let's find out!

10 Jennifer Carpenter - $8 Million

Jennifer Carpenter has had quite the success in the entertainment business! The actress debuted back in 2002, however, she didn't gain nationwide prominence up until her 2004 appearance in the hit comedy, White Chicks, where she played the role of Lisa alongside Busy Phillips, Jessica Cauffiel, and of course, the Wayans brothers.

Carpenter later gained immense fame on hit shows such as Limitless, Dexter, and The Enemy Within, all while landing roles in many films, including Quarantine, allowing her to amass a net worth of $8 million.

9 Jennifer Coolidge - $8 Million

Jennifer Coolidge is one actress that we cannot get enough of! The star is known for her iconic voice, which has been portrayed via characters in films such as The Cinderella Story, Legally Blonde, and of course, American Pie!

Jennifer also gained prominence through her many TV roles, which include appearances on 2 Broke Girls, Joey, and Sex And The City. Over the course of Coolidge's three-decade-long career, it's only fitting that she now revels in her success and $8 million fortune!

8 Jennifer Grey - $10 Million

Jennifer Grey is without a doubt an icon! The beloved star took on the role of Frances Houseman, also known as Baby, in the hit 1987 film, Dirty Dancing, in which she appeared alongside the late, Patrick Swayze.

The actress reigned throughout the 80s and 90s, landing roles in Ferris Bueller's Day Off, Red Dawn, If The Shoe Fits, and Wind, to name a few. While Grey remains a legend for her film work, the star isn't as active in the industry today, however, she's got a lovely $10 million net worth to keep her more than comfy!

7 Jennifer Love Hewitt - $22 Million

Jennifer Love-Hewitt first came to prominence following her success on the hit series, Part Of Five, where she played the role of Sara Reeves Merrin. Jennifer later landed a role in the horror film, I Know What You Did Last Summer, which catapulted her career to great heights.

She later became one of the biggest actresses of the 90s and early 2000s, landing parts in Garfield, The Ghost Whisperer, Criminal Minds, and not to mention her decade-long music career, all of which have earned the star a whopping $22 million net worth.

6 Jennifer Hudson - $25 Million

Jennifer Hudson made a name for herself on the talent series, American Idol back in 2004 on the third season. While she was eliminated far earlier than expected, Hudson went on to land the role of Effie White in the movie musical, Dreamgirls, which scored Jennifer an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress.

Hudson went on to appear in films such as Sex And The City, however, it was her powerhouse vocals that skyrocketed her career to the top of the music charts. The singer recently collaborated with Mariah Carey and Ariana Grande on Carey's classic Christmas hit, 'Oh Santa'. With a career as expansive as hers, it is no surprise that Jennifer Hudson has amassed a net worth of $25 million. 

5 Jennifer Tilly - $25 Million

Jennifer Tilly is a Hollywood icon! The actress is known for being quite the on-screen hustler, appearing in over 120 credited roles spanning from 1983 to today.

Tilly gained fame and fortune following her appearance in the 1994 classic Woody Allen film, Bullets Over Broadway. Jennifer then went on to appear in the Chucky franchise, in the role of Tiffanie Valentine, which has certainly advanced her career in many ways, all while amassing an impressive fortune of $25 million!

4 Jennifer Garner - $80 Million

Jennifer Garner has been in the acting game since her 1995 debut, however, the actress found herself at the top of the leaderboard following her time on the hit series, Alias. During this time, Garner landed hit roles in 13 Going On 30, Daredevil, Elektra, and more recent additions such as Peppermint, and Love, Simon.

Today, Jennifer is worth a whopping $80 million, which she is now putting towards her newest farm venture. In 2017, the actress began to serve as the chief brand officer and co-founder of Once Upon a Farm with original masterminds Cassandra Curtis and Ari Raz.

3 Jennifer Lawrence - $160 Million

Jennifer Lawrence has not only spent the least amount of time in the industry compared to the other Jennifer's, but she is also the youngest! After gaining prominence following her role in the 2010 film, Winter's Bone; scoring Lawrence a 2011 Best Actress Oscar nomination, Jennifer became one of the biggest stars in Hollywood.

She landed the role of Katniss Everdeen in the Hunger Games trilogy, and X-Men series, all while being cast in critically acclaimed films such as Silver Lining's Playbook, Joy, and America Hustle, all of which earned her an Oscar nom each, with Silver Lining's being her first and sole win! Since her start, Lawrence has amassed a net worth of $160 million, which is only set to grow.

2 Jennifer Aniston - $300 Million

Jennifer Aniston came about back in the late 80s, however, it wasn't until she landed the role of Rachel Green on Friends that she became a household name. From everyone wanting her hairstyle, to simply wanting to be her, Jennifer Aniston was without a doubt the "it" actress and remains so to this very day.

After her success on the TV series, Aniston moved on to film, where she has appeared in movies alongside Adam Sandler, Vince Vaughn, Jim Carrey, Gerard Butler, and Jason Sudeikis, to name a few, proving her power within the comedy and drama genre. Luckily for Aniston, her success in the industry has allowed her to amass a $300 million fortune!

1 Jennifer Lopez - $400 Million

Jennifer Lopez has certainly cemented her status as one of the biggest triple threats in the entertainment business! The star is known for her singing, dancing, and of course, acting. JLo, who has had an endless array of hit songs, has found herself on the big screen throughout her entire career.

From her appearances in Maid In Manhattan, Selena, Monster-In-Law, all while selling millions upon millions of records, it is no shock that Lopez has built herself a $400 million empire.
